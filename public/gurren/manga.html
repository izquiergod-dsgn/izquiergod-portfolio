Manga Details

The Gurren Lagann manga is an adaptation of the anime series, featuring the same over-the-top mecha action and emotional storytelling that made the anime a classic.

Follow Simon's journey from a timid digger to the leader of Team Dai-Gurren as they fight against the Spiral King and his Beastmen army.

The manga expands on some scenes from the anime and offers a slightly different perspective on the epic story of humanity's fight for freedom.

Author: Kazuki Nakashima
Artist: Kotaro Mori
Volumes: 6
Published: 2007-2008
Publisher: ASCII Media Works

About the Anime

Tengen Toppa Gurren Lagann is a 2007 mecha anime series that follows Simon and Kamina as they fight against the Spiral King to free humanity from underground villages. Known for its over-the-top action, emotional storytelling, and iconic quotes, it's considered one of the greatest mecha anime of all time.
