About the Anime

Tengen Toppa Gurren Lagann is a 2007 mecha anime series that follows Simon and Kamina as they fight against the Spiral King to free humanity from underground villages. Known for its over-the-top action, emotional storytelling, and iconic quotes, it's considered one of the greatest mecha anime of all time.
