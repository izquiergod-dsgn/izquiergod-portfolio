PIERCE THE HEAVENS!

In a future where humanity is forced to live in underground villages by the tyrannical Spiral King, a young digger named Simon discovers a mysterious drill-shaped key that awakens a small mecha called Lagann.

Together with his brash brother-figure Kamina and sharpshooter Yoko, Simon forms Team Dai-Gurren to fight against the Beastmen and reach the surface world. Their journey takes them from underground caves to the farthest reaches of space in an epic battle for humanity's freedom.

Believe in the you that believes in yourself! This is the story of a man who continues to fight against fate...

About the Anime

Tengen Toppa Gurren Lagann is a 2007 mecha anime series that follows Simon and Kamina as they fight against the Spiral King to free humanity from underground villages. Known for its over-the-top action, emotional storytelling, and iconic quotes, it's considered one of the greatest mecha anime of all time.
